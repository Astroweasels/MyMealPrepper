About My Meal Calendar

A personal project for meal planning, learning, and sharing.

This app started when my wife suggested we plan our meals for the month.
Instead of reaching for a notebook, I decided it was a good excuse to build something useful — and learn Angular and AWS in the process.

What began as a small side project turned into a fully deployed web application
with a cloud-hosted frontend and automated deployment pipeline.
