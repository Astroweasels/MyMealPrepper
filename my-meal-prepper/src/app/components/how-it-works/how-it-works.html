How It Works
  • Tap or click any meal slot to add your own recipe or edit details.
  • Tap or click Generate random meals to fill empty slots.
  • If you don’t like a meal, tap or click the re-roll icon to pick a new one.
  • Tap or click the mini print icon to print just that meal’s recipe.
  • Tap or click the grocery list to edit it manually.
  • Want fewer meal slots? Use the "Meal times" dropdown to show or hide meal types.
  • Tap or click the print button to print your meal schedule and grocery list.
