How It Works
  • Click any meal to edit ingredients or instructions.
  • Click Generate random meals to fill the rest.
  • If you don't like a meal, click the re-roll icon to generate a new one.
  • Click the mini print icon to print just that meal's recipe.
  • Click the grocery list to edit it manually.
  • Don't want to plan out every meal? Use the "Meal times" dropdown to toggle which meal times will show.
  • Click the print button to print your meal schedule and grocery list.
